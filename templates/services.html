My Services

  • Structural Analysis & Design

    Designing and analyzing structures such as buildings, foundations, bridges and other infrastructure to ensure they meet governing code, safety, performance, and durability requirements.

  • Structural Inspections

    Conducting structural inspections and assessments of existing buildings and infrastructure to identify potential issues, assess structural integrity, and recommend repairs or upgrades as required.

  • Construction Support Services

    Providing technical support during the construction phase, including reviewing shop drawings, responding to contractor RFIs, and conducting site visits to ensure compliance with design specifications.

  • Building Information Modeling (BIM)

    Utilizing BIM technology to create detailed 3D models of structures, facilitating collaboration between project stakeholders, improving design accuracy, and optimizing construction processes.

  • Project Management

    Overseeing construction projects from start to completion, including scheduling, resource allocation, coordination with stakeholders and quality control to ensure project delivery within scope, time, and budget constraints.

  • Cost Estimation and Compliance

    Developing cost estimates for construction projects, including material costs, labor costs, and other expenses, and ensuring compliance with budget constraints and financial considerations throughout the project lifecycle.
